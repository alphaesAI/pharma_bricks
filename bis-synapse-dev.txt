WORKFLOW (How data flows into Synapse)
================================================================================

Databricks Platinum Delta Tables
         ↓
    JDBC Connection (SetupSQLConnectionJDBC.py)
         ↓
    StreamGoldAndPlatinumToSynapse.py
         ↓
    Azure Synapse Dedicated SQL Pool
         ↓
    Platinum Tables (platinum_*) ← FINAL ANALYTICS LAYER
         ↓
    Power BI / Reporting Tools
